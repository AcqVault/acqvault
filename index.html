Why AcqVault

Acquisition research
without the friction.

Navigating acquisition.gov tabs, Federal Register alerts, and scattered PDFs shouldn't be your job. Researching what they say should be.

⌕
Full-text search
Search across the RFO, R-DFARS, FAR Companion, DoD FMR, AFI 63-138, and more — simultaneously. Find the regulation by how you'd describe it, not how it's filed.
◎
Status at a glance
Every document is tagged — Open for comment, Interim, Class Deviation, Proposed, or Final. Know the rule's standing before you cite it.
↻
Always current
Indexed monthly from the Federal Register API and acquisition.gov. No stale PDFs. No bookmarks that die mid-acquisition.
⊘
No AI summaries
You get the authoritative source text — not a model's interpretation of it. AcqVault is a research library, not a chatbot.
⊞
Multi-source filtering
Select one or several sources simultaneously — RFO, R-DFARS, FAR Companion, and more. Stack your filters and narrow by status too.
◈
Free & open access
No cost, no subscriptions. AcqVault is a community resource built for the AF acquisition workforce.
